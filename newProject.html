About

We are engineering specialists and independent consultants with poly-skills and technology at heart believers with over many years of end to end software development experience working with companies and individuals to solve challenging problems with a robust, pragmatic approach and cutting edge technology. We believe that at tech.at.core, you never get bored with the work and challenges you will have.
